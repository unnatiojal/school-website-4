About School

School name - SURAJMULL JAIN D.A.V PUBLIC SCHOOL

In the long and ever-expanding chain of DAV institutions this school was started on 1st July 1987, in the cherished memory of reverend Seth SURAJMULL JAIN under
the management of DAV Trust, New Delhi, with an objective of discovering and developing the special aptitude and hidden potentialities of the students of this tribal locality.
The school is running at Tata Road which is having the classes from LKG to Std XII. (Commerce and Science stream) and have all the modern equipments and facilities to match
the need of the students.

Campus Life

School field

The school field is for kids to play sports. It is mainly used for Physical Training. Sometimes, it is used to play Basketball, Badminton, Cricket, Volleyball, kho-kho, kabaddi and football, and for the primary students there is sand-pit, swing, slide and seesaw.

School Library

The school has Library Facilities with more than nine thousand Library Books of Student interest. This facility is available to all bonafide students of the School during the School hours. All classes from class one onwards are provided with at least one library period per week. Students are not allowed to bring their personal books to the Library. Students are allowed to borrow books on their own library cards only

Mini Prize Distribution Ceremony
Mini Prize Distribution Ceremony 2019

The prize distribution in our school was held in the first week of March.It was held in the school field. The school was cleaned and decorated. Many preparations were made. The guardians of the students were invited. The prizes were placed on the table.The function started with a prayer song by two girls. Then the girls staged a dance item. Then a drama was staged.The chief guest then distributed the prizes. The prize-winners received prizes and cheers. At the end, the chief guest made a short speech and congratulated the prize-winners. The Principal thanked the chief guest. The chief guest departed. The function came to an end.
